About

Digital Art

My digital portfolio mainly consists of pixel art, drawn with a tablet and mouse.

A collection of 63 portraits of original character designs and characters from other media. These were drawn over 3 years. For these portraits, I really had to think outside the box due to the limited 96x80 box size and the limit of a 15-color indexed palette.

A test with Adobe Illustrator to make a Low-Poly coffee mug.

Projects

I'm the project and art lead of a fangame, Eternal Winter. For this project, I have made portraits, animations, map animations, UI, icons, and more. In addition, I've also worked on creating assets for several community fangames, including Call of the Armor and Bells of Byelen.

This project started as an opportunity for me to learn pixel art portraits, animations, icons, while dealing with the constraints like pixel size, box size, and restricted palette order.
